Marketing for Brown Sugar Eatery

Brown Sugar Eatery is a petite cafe based in Bayan Lepas, Pulau Pinang. I was assigned to this cafe to perform social media management, digital marketing and to create content for it. I have find myself more skilled and experienced after handling this task in terms of knowing the target audience, how to reach our target audiences and what are the best ways to optimize our contents for the general and target audiences. This had also taught me well on how to utilize social media's advertising tools to boost up engagements for the content.I find myself easy to understand the algortihm of social media such as Instagram when it comes to promoting and digital marketing.
