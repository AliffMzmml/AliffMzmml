MY PHOTOGRAPHY JOURNEY CINEMATICA

  • @ALIFFMZMML

I spent a lot of time trying to craft my own style and identity in the photography world. And those times that I spent was to learn about composition, lighting, colour grading and how to convey emotions in my photograph. All of the studies has led me to make my photographs look cinematic, hence the name "CINEMATICA"         


My achievements in photography.


I was raised in Penang and my goal was to make Penang look beautiful, moody and cinematic through my eyes and it is something I want to share with the public, at the same time promoting Penang. My series of photographs was featured twice in an article from a local organization called Penang Foodie. First one was "Deep into the night" and the second was "Penang in the rain"
